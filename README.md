# TV Licensing – No Licence Required Letter (UK)

TV Licensing – No Licence Required Letter (UK)  The following template proved effective in my own circumstances. It is shared for information only and is not legal advice. If it applies to your situation, simply replace <Your Address> and <Date> with your own details before sending.
This repository contains a simple template letter for UK residents who **do not require a TV licence** because they do not watch or record live television broadcasts and do not use BBC iPlayer.

The template is based on a letter that proved effective in my own circumstances in bringing unwanted correspondence to an end. It is shared as a public resource for others in a similar position.

## Who this is for

This letter may be suitable if:

* You do **not** watch or record live television on any channel or service.
* You do **not** use BBC iPlayer.
* The statements made in the letter are true for your household.

If your circumstances are different, you should not use this template without adapting it accordingly.

## Disclaimer

This repository is provided for **general information only** and does **not** constitute legal advice. It is simply a template based on one individual's experience. You remain responsible for ensuring that any letter you send is accurate and reflects your own circumstances.

## How to use

1. Open the template letter.
2. Replace the placeholders with your own address and the current date.
3. Read the letter carefully to ensure every statement is true.
4. Send it to TV Licensing if appropriate.
